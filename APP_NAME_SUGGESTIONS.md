## My Personal Top 3 Picks

### 🥇 **FlowGood**
- **Why**: Modern, positive, works for both givers and receivers
- **Strength**: Single word, easy to spell/remember, inspirational
- **Tagline**: "Let Good Things Flow to Your Community"
- **Perfect for**: Tech-savvy users, young professionals, social impact focused

### 🥈 **TableShare**
- **Why**: Warm, inclusive, community feeling
- **Strength**: Universal metaphor (everyone knows "sitting at the table")
- **Tagline**: "Everyone Deserves a Place at the Table"
- **Perfect for**: Community organizations, NGOs, universal appeal

### 🥉 **SupplyAid**
- **Why**: Professional, clear business value
- **Strength**: Business-friendly but mission-driven
- **Tagline**: "Supply Meets Need, Every Time"
- **Perfect for**: B2B adoption, corporate partnerships, provider confidence

## Questions to Help Choose:

1. **Who is your primary audience?**
   - Tech-savvy startups & young restaurants? → FlowGood
   - Community-focused NGOs & shelters? → TableShare
   - Mix of professionals & nonprofits? → SupplyAid

2. **What feeling should it evoke?**
   - Optimism & modern energy? → FlowGood
   - Warmth & belonging? → TableShare
   - Professional & trustworthy? → SupplyAid

3. **What's your growth strategy?**
   - Start with restaurants, scale to corporate? → SupplyAid
   - Start grassroots, grow community-first? → TableShare
   - Modern tech platform marketing? → FlowGood

## Implementation Notes

Whatever name you choose, we'll need to update:

1. **Header** → App title + tagline
2. **Page titles** → "Available on [AppName]" etc
3. **Buttons** → CTA messaging aligned with name
4. **Marketing** → Benefits tied to the name's promise
5. **Social/Email** → Consistent branding

All changes can be made in a few minutes once you decide!
